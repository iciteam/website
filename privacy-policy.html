Who we are

Our company name is Innovative Compliance, Inc. Our website address is iciteam.com.

What information do we track and how do we track it?

Just like many other websites on the World Wide Web, we use Google Analytics.

Google Analytics is software, provided by google, for website owners. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous tracking data from the visitors on websites. Cookies are small files that are transferred to your hard drive on your computer after you visit a website using cookies or using third party software that uses cookies, like our website. Cookies files are used for online record-keeping purposes. Google Analytics uses these cookies to record data including:

  • What kind of device you are using to access the internet
  • What website you came from
  • How long you stayed on our website
  • And more.

What do we do with this data?

After Google Analytics collects data from our website, we analyze the data in order to understand our audience better. This data is used to make decisions such as how we present our content, what we write, how to improve user experience, and marketing purposes.

How do we store your data?

We do not directly store your data from coolies. Your data is stored in Google Analytics. We simply log into our Google Analytics account and look at the anonymous aggregated data from all of our users. Please follow the link to find out how Google Analytics protects your data.

How do I opt out of Google Analytics tracking?

If you don’t want to be tracked with cookies from Google Analytics, you can Stop Google Analytics Tracking Software
