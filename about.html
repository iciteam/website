Chief Executive Officer

Amara Saengwattana-Hughes

Amara graduated from RKHU in Thailand before entering the hospitality industry and becoming Director of Operations at a large international hotel. After moving to the United States and raising a family, Amara has dedicated her efforts to growing Innovative into a state-of-the-art pipeline consulting and professional services firm.

Vice President of Energy Infrastructure

Tyler Hughes, EIT

Tyler joined ICI in 2018 and has since led the company through multiple compliance and high-pressure replacement projects for utility and midstream clients across the country. Tyler graduated from the University of California, Davis with a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering and has years of experience in the oil & gas and construction industries.
